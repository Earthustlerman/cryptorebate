Questions

Straight answers.

Is this actually free?
Yes. You pay nothing. The exchange pays us a commission for sending them traders. We split that commission with you. There is no catch and no hidden cost.
Do I have to change how I trade?
Not at all. You trade exactly as you do now. The only difference is you signed up through our link, which means we earn a commission that we share back with you monthly.
How do I receive my cashback?
Each month we calculate your rebate based on your trading volume and send your share in crypto directly. You'll receive payment details when you join.
I already have an account on Bybit or Binance. Can I still join?
Unfortunately affiliate tracking only works for new accounts created through our referral link. If you already have an account, you'd need to open a new one to participate.
Is this available to South Africans?
Yes — this is built specifically with South African traders in mind. All supported exchanges accept South African users and the cashback is paid in crypto which you can convert to ZAR.
